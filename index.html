Resorts

Kamari

Cool Kamari sits on Santorini’s east coast, just north of soaring Mount Mesa Vouno. This traditional Greek village is lined with an black shore and is one of the island’s buzzier spots.

Oia

Looking for Santorini’s most iconic village? Ta-da, its Oia. White churches, whirling windmills and beautiful blue domes hug the rugged cliffside creating a spot that’s truly mesmerising.

Go somewhere

Imerovigli

Upscale Imerovigli village sits on a clifftop overlooking Santorini’s caldera islands. The terraces of its bars, fine-dining restaurants, and hotels, are popular sunset viewing spots.
